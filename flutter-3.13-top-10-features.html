TextField Scanning on iOS


TextField scanning on iOS is a new feature in Flutter 3.13 that lets in customers to test text without delay from the device camera into a TextField widget. This can be useful for coming into text quickly and easily, such as whilst getting into a CAPTCHA or barcode.

To use TextField scanning on iOS, you will need to add the camera permission to your app's Info.Plist report. You can even need to add a TextFieldScanner widget for your app's layout and set its controller belongings to the identical TextEditingController item that you used for the TextField widget.

When the user taps at the TextFieldScanner widget, a digicam view may be displayed. The person can then test a barcode or QR code. Once the test is whole, the scanned text can be inserted into the TextField widget.

TextField scanning on iOS is a powerful new characteristic that allow you to to enhance the consumer enjoy of your Flutter apps. By making it clean for users to enter textual content into your apps, you could store them effort and time.

Here are a few examples of ways you could use TextField scanning on iOS on your app:

  • Allow users to experiment a barcode to quick add a product to their buying cart.
  • Allow users to experiment a QR code to log in to your app or get right of entry to a particular page.
  • Allow users to test a barcode on a shipping label to song the status of their order.
  • Allow users to scan a QR code on a menu to view the eating place's internet site or see the fees of objects.

The possibilities are infinite!
